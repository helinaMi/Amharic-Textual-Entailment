    Textual entailment Classifier Models.

    RSSM model follows an approach of compare and aggregate to identify the model performance. Experiments were conduct to compare our model against the models that follow the similar approaches using our dataset. Models used for comparison are the following.

    • Word by Word attention(WBWA) by Tim Rocktasche.
    • Compare and Aggrgate by Wang(CAA), Shuohang, Jing Jiang.
    • Bilateral multi perspective by Zhiguo Wang.
    • Reverse side sentence matching, with skip-gram Embedding layer(RSSM(SG)).
    • Full Reverse side sentence matching, with sub-word Embedding layer(RSSM(SW).

    From the training and validation accuracy RSSM with SW has showed a better performance in training and validation dataset from the compared models. This indicates, Amharic language befits from the sub-word embedding in input layer, adding a mean pooling approach in sentence comparison layer and the matching performed in the reverse direction (the hypothesis against the premises).
